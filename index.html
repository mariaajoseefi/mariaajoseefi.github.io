A Little About Me

Where I'm From

I was born and raised in Mexico. Growing up, my fascination with technology started early, fueled by my curiosity to understand how things worked. Now, I’m on a mission to make tech accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

What I Do Now

I design and develop user-centric applications, collaborating with cross-functional teams to bring ideas to life. I’m passionate about bridging the gap between technology and design, ensuring that every interaction is meaningful.

What’s Important to Me

Making technology more human-centric is my goal. I believe in the power of design to change lives and the importance of creating products that genuinely help users.
